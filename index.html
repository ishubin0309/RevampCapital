If residential real estate is everywhere, why is it hard to invest in at scale or speed?

Institutional investors target just 4% of the available residential real estate market

Today’s investors deploy capital through large multi-family housing (MFH), build-to-rent (BTR) or secondary portfolio acquisitions. These solutions are expensive, exposed to development or concentration risk, occupy secondary locations and can take years to generate income.

We unlock access to 96% of the residential market ignored by investors – the single-family market.

We combine breakthrough technology with local expertise to aggregate single-family rental (SFR) and small multi-family assets into attractively priced, well-located, income generating portfolios at scale, allowing us to deploy capital and generate stabilised income at a speed unseen before in Europe.
